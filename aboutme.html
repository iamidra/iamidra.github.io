👋 Hello There

I'm Idra Mkumbanya, a Computer Networks and Information Security Engineering student at the University of Dodoma, Tanzania. I'm passionate about building secure, modern web applications and understanding how systems can be protected from the ground up.

I started web development out of curiosity and quickly realised how deeply it intersects with security. Today I work across the full stack — from designing interfaces to hardening backends — always with security in mind. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring Linux, reading about networking, or tinkering with CTF challenges.
